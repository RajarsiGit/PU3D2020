Welcome

About the conference

The purpose of this conference is to create a platform for the young scientist and students from various institutions across the country to gain knowledge about recent trends in the drug development process and prepare themselves to identify new targets to overcome the existing disadvantages of conventional therapy and develop new molecules and diagnostics based on the targets. The conference will cover a range of topics in Drug Discovery and development.

Thrust areas

  • Drug Discovery and Development
  • Pre-Clinical and Clinical Research
  • Role of Nanotechnology in Drug Discovery
  • Bioinformatics and Pharmacogenomics in Drug Discovery
  • Molecular Modelling In Drug Designing
  • Bio-markers and Proteomics in Drug Discovery
  • Novel Drug Delivery System
  • Recent developments in Biochemistry & Molecular Biology and career advice
